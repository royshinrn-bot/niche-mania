Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026  ·  Effective date: April 25, 2026

Mountain Creative Studio ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Life by Shift mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Life by Shift does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The App does not require account registration, login, or any form of identification.

2. Data Storage

All data entered into the App (schedules, shift types, income estimates, and notes) is stored exclusively on your device using your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services. No data is shared with or sold to any third party.

4. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from users of any age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

5. Security

Since all data remains on your device and no data is transmitted to external servers, your information is inherently protected from external breaches.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
